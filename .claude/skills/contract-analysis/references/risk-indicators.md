# Risk Scoring

## CRITICAL (score 5)
Immediate legal/financial risk — attorney review before signing.
- Unlimited liability (no carve-outs)
- IP fully transferred to counterparty
- No termination right for convenience
- Arbitration required for claims > $500K without right to injunctive relief

## HIGH (score 4)
Significant risk — flag for negotiation.
- Liability cap below contract value
- Auto-renewal > 12 months without notice window
- One-sided unilateral amendment rights
- Non-compete > 24 months or worldwide scope
- Missing Art. 28 DPA (GDPR contracts)

## MEDIUM (score 3)
Standard risk — review recommended.
- Liquidated damages present
- Exclusivity provisions
- Non-solicitation clauses
- Missing force majeure (non-English law)

## LOW (score 2)
Minor risk — informational.
- Standard payment terms (Net 30/60)
- Notice periods < 30 days

## INFO (score 1)
No risk.
- Boilerplate definitions
- Recitals, preamble
